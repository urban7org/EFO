PARTNER UPDATE  ·  SPRING 2026

The Bow Is Being Drawn

A letter from the founder on what God is building in Englewood

 

"I have stood with too many families around caskets that should have been graduation stages."

Friends,

The moment that changed everything for me wasn't dramatic in the way you'd expect. I was sitting in our after-school program, watching our dedicated tutors try to inspire children who were already exhausted by a failing system. And a thought hit me that I couldn't shake: we have been trying to change a trajectory that was already set. Every day, a broken system was solidifying a direction we were scrambling to reverse.

What if our calling was not just to fix — but to aim?

What if our ministry could become the Archer — the steady hand that chooses a better target, draws the bow with discipline, and ensures a truer aim from the very start?

That vision is Archer Christian Academy. And I'm writing today because we are closer than we've ever been.

THE ANSWER

Not a Reaction. A Proactive Answer.

Archer Christian Academy is not another program, not another intervention, not another attempt to fix what's already broken. It is permanent infrastructure for human flourishing — a Christ-centered, classical school that will form children from the very start. Shaping how they think. Strengthening who they are. And launching them as leaders who bring renewal to their families, their churches, and their city.

THE PROOF

This Model Works. We've Seen It.

We are not starting from scratch. Through the Spreading Hope Network — launched from Hope Academy to help other communities replicate what God did in Minneapolis — we are connected to a national movement that has been helping plant Christ-centered schools in underserved cities for nearly a decade. And our model school, Hope Academy, is living proof of what's possible:
