Synthesizing the Need

The data converges to make a powerful, undeniable case for establishing a new Classical Christian school at a location like 67th and Union.

  • 1

    Clear Academic Need

    With local public school proficiency rates lagging far behind state and even city averages, Englewood families urgently need access to academically rigorous alternatives to ensure their children can thrive.

  • 2

    An Educational Desert

    There are zero Classical Christian schools in Englewood and a profound lack of them on the entire South Side. A new school would not be competing; it would be filling a complete void.

  • 3

    High-Impact Location

    A neighborhood-based school provides a safe, walkable option for local families, reducing transportation barriers that often prevent access to quality education outside the immediate area. It can become a true anchor for the community.
